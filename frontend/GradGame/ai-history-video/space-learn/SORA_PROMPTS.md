# Sora prompts — “Space Adventure: Learn the Planets”

Use one **30-second** clip per planet (adjust `OPENAI_VIDEO_SECONDS` / model limits to match your API).  
After export, name files e.g. `mercury.mp4` … `neptune.mp4` and set `videoUrl` in `src/data/planets.js` to `/api/video/mercury.mp4` etc.

---

## Mercury

Create a 30-second animated educational video for children.

A cute talking rocket with a friendly face (big eyes, expressive) is the narrator.

- 0–5s: Rocket flies toward Mercury near the Sun  
- 5–10s: Show Mercury (rocky, gray, craters)  
- 10–20s: Rotate around Mercury, show heat glow  
- 20–30s: Rocket gestures while explaining  

**Narration:**  
“This is Mercury, the closest planet to the Sun! It is very hot during the day and very cold at night. Mercury is small and rocky.”
