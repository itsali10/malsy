# Space Adventure Story – how the astronaut storyteller should sound

Use simple words for kids (ages 6–12).
Sound like a magical story adventure, not a dry lecture — excited, curious, warm.
The astronaut speaks to the viewer as a guide on a journey through the planets.
Use “Imagine…” or “Next we visit…” and short, fun facts at each stop.
Ask one or two simple questions to keep kids engaged.
Pause mentally between ideas (good for future voiceover).
End with a friendly sign-off (e.g. “See you on the next mission!”).
