# Space lesson videos

Put your exported lesson MP4 files here and point to them from `space-adventure/js/config.js`:

| Scene        | Suggested file | `videoUrl` example        |
|-------------|----------------|---------------------------|
| Intro       | `intro.mp4`    | `"/space-media/intro.mp4"` |
| Mercury & Venus | `pair1.mp4` | `"/space-media/pair1.mp4"` |
| Earth & Mars    | `pair2.mp4` | `"/space-media/pair2.mp4"` |
| Jupiter & Saturn | `pair3.mp4` | `"/space-media/pair3.mp4"` |
| Uranus & Neptune | `pair4.mp4` | `"/space-media/pair4.mp4"` |

If `videoUrl` is `null`, the lesson uses a short **demo clip** so students can test the flow without your files yet.
