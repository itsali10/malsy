# Sora prompts (Space Adventure)

Copy from the app per scene, or edit `public/space-adventure/js/config.js` (`soraPrompt` on each scene).

---

## Scene 1 — Intro

Colorful cinematic cartoon for kids ages 6–12. A cute friendly astronaut in a small playful rocket launches from Earth at sunrise. The rocket flies past soft clouds into a starry sky with glowing stars and gentle nebula colors. The astronaut waves at the camera with big expressive eyes. Calm, magical, Pixar-like 3D animation, bright colors, not scary. Short clip, smooth camera, family-friendly.

## Scene 2 — Mercury

Educational cartoon space scene for children. The same cute astronaut mascot flies close to a small gray rocky planet with glowing cracks and harsh sunlight — Mercury. The Sun looks large and bright. Stylized, non-photorealistic, colorful 3D animation. Show the planet slowly rotating. Warm, curious mood. No text on screen. Short clip.

## Scene 3 — Venus

Cartoon space adventure for kids. The friendly astronaut floats near planet Venus — thick swirling golden-yellow clouds, soft light, beautiful and mysterious. Stylized planet, glowing atmosphere, dreamy lighting. Pixar-style 3D, vibrant, calm and magical. No scary elements. Short educational clip.

## Scene 4 — Earth

Heartwarming cartoon scene for children. View of Earth from space: blue oceans, white clouds, green land, sunlit curve of the planet. The small cartoon rocket silhouette passes by with a tiny sparkle. Soft glowing stars in the background. Emotional but gentle — “our home in the cosmos.” Colorful 3D animation, not photorealistic NASA footage. Short clip.

## Scene 5 — Mars

Fun educational cartoon for kids. The astronaut explores near Mars — red-orange desert surface, dust in the air, distant mountains, two small moons in the sky. Stylized, chunky shapes, big colors. Adventure mood, curious and friendly. 3D cartoon style, no horror. Short clip.

## Scene 6 — Jupiter

Spectacular cartoon space scene for children. Huge planet Jupiter fills part of the frame — swirling bands of clouds, the Great Red Spot as a big oval storm, small astronaut rocket for scale. Colorful, exaggerated, awe-inspiring but cute. Soft lighting, Pixar-like 3D. No text. Short clip.

## Scene 7 — Saturn

Magical cartoon scene for kids. Planet Saturn with wide glowing icy rings that shimmer and slowly rotate. The friendly astronaut floats nearby in awe. Starfield background, soft purples and golds. Dreamy, beautiful, educational tone. Stylized 3D animation, family-friendly. Short clip.

## Scene 8 — Uranus

Whimsical space cartoon for children. Pale blue-green planet Uranus with a smooth surface and faint rings, tilted funny like a ball rolling in space. Tiny cute rocket passes by. Cool colors, sparkly stars, gentle humor. 3D cartoon, not realistic documentary. Short clip.

## Scene 9 — Neptune

Epic but kid-safe cartoon space scene. Deep blue planet Neptune with dark storms and fast-moving clouds; lightning hints but soft and stylized, not scary. Distant Sun as a tiny bright dot. Astronaut rocket silhouette for scale. Mysterious, adventurous ending feel. Colorful 3D animation. Short clip.
